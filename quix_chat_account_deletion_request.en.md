Use this page to request deletion of your Quix Chat account and associated personal data.

## Fastest Option: Delete in the App

If you can still access your account, use in-app deletion:

1. Open `Quix Chat`.
2. Go to `Settings`.
3. Select `Delete account`.
4. Confirm the deletion prompt by typing `DELETE`.

This is the fastest and most reliable method because it verifies account ownership from an authenticated session.

## Can’t Access the App? Submit a Web Request

If you cannot sign in, you can still request deletion using either route below.

- Email: `mailto:[Insert privacy email]?subject=Quix%20Chat%20Account%20Deletion%20Request`
- Request form URL: `[Insert public deletion request form URL]`

In your request, include:

- the phone number used for Quix Chat (with country code);
- your approximate last sign-in date;
- your country/region;
- a statement that you want account deletion.

For security, we may request additional verification (for example, verification tied to your phone number) before deleting the account.

## What Happens After a Valid Request

After verification, Quix Chat will process account deletion and associated data deletion/de-identification according to applicable law and our retention rules.

Data may be retained for limited periods where required for:

- legal obligations;
- security and abuse prevention;
- fraud prevention;
- dispute handling;
- backup and audit controls.

## Processing Timeline

Quix Chat aims to complete verified deletion requests without undue delay. Some records may take additional time to clear from backups or legally required retention systems.
